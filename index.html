Rules

Card Effects :

Skip Card (S) : Enemy player misses a turn.

Reverse Card (R) : Order of play switches directions.

Draw Two (+2) : Enemy player draws two cards and misses a turn.

Wild Card (W) : Player declares the next color to be matched and enemy misses a turn.

Wild Draw Four (+4W) : Player declares the next color to be matched, enemy player draws four cards and misses a turn.

Player 1 plays first.

If first table card is Wild Card then Player 1 decides the color before Player 2 takes cards.

If first table card is Draw Two then Player 2 draws two cards and starts with 9 cards total.
